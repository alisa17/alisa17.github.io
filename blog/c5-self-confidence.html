What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

When you are aware and mindful towards your emotional experience that is taking place, then you stand a chance of understanding yourself and managing the situation. The 'raging river' metaphor really tells it all. The moment you can see a raging river, you are already rising above it. Thus seeing it is the first and an important step to the solution. Self awareness really is just emotional mindfulness. Where mindfulness is paying undivided attention to every single experience you are having, i.e. focusing on task at hand, self awareness is like that but more centered around the emotional side of your existence and includes things like evaluating yourself, knowing your strengths and weaknesses and so on. It really is knowing yourself in general as well as understanding yourself at the present moment, and that means being mindful to self.
