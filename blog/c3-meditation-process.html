On Meditation Process

How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

I had to give up some ideas in the name of time boxing. In my opinion good process suggests timely delivery so this was something i had to keep mind.

What new things have you learnt about meditation?

The concept of meta attention and how it is the balancing act that lies at the core of meditating especially when it comes to beginners. I learnt that there is no such thing as bad meditation, even if you failed to not let your mind drift a 1000 times and had to correct yourself a 1000 times, you already did very well and benefited your wellbing and happiness.
I like the idea of meditation being the exercise for the brain and the grandmother mind analogy.

What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2?

I have dabbled in meditation before and was vaguely aware of everything I read in the chapter. The book however does provide an elegant summary that is tailored for IT workers and I like that it allows me to systemize my knowledge of meditation practices.

Did any of the suggested meditation techniques stand out to you?

Nothing mindblowingly new in the techniques.Breathing and grandmother mind are good techniques however.

Any other musings?

Very keen to get my brain back to its default state and allow happiness to happen through meditation :)
