Analogies and Definitions

What is grid based design?

Grid based design refers to content being organised on a page as if elemnts are all part of a grid that follows a top to bottom, left to right pattern.

Whats all the hype about responsive webpages?

The hype has to do with an ever increasing number of smart phone and tablet users as well as there being many different browsers and computer screens. Responsive design means the kind of design that is convenient to use for all users regardless their choice of device or browser, i.e. the kind of design that responds to the user's needs.

What is semantic structure?

Semantic structure means structure of tags that reflect the semantic meaning of the element
