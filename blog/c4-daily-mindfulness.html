What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

What I learnt is that oftentimes we don't give each oher anywhere near as much attention as we deserve when it comes to listening. I ejoyed being the listener and having my attention 100% absorbed into what my partner was saying. I am convinced mindful conversation is rewarding to either party and I am going to try it again with a variety of people I might be talking to. I do think that not paying attention while conversing is really robbing yourself of the experience that is human interaction and I want for all of my conversations to be as mindful of possible from this point on.
