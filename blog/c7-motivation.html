How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?

Life looks pretty good through the lens of all three provided:

  • purpose is the underlining driving force and core principle behind your actions
  • passion is the state which you enter when you are working on things that matter to you
  • rock-star pleasure is something you don't chase but indulge in occasionally but not too often you get sick of it and it loses its appeal

My core values include:

  • Freedom of expression and creativity
  • Professional and personal growth and progress: increasing standards for self and for quality of my work, becoming better than yesterday self
  • Acquiring practical knowledge and knowledge sharing
  • Love and kindness

What do you stand for?

  • Equality
  • Inclusion
  • Growth and progress

How did you find the resilience ideas and meditation exercise?

Being resilient to failure is important but I also feel doesn't require a lot of deep understanding as the concept is pretty easy to grasp and is a natural behaviour at least for me and many other people I know - if plan A fails you move on to plan B, if that fails you start working on plan C. Never take it personally, never blame it on others - it's noone's fault, you did the best you could at the time, just move on to your next plan or look for new solutions and you should be good.
